Destroit-style hotdog

It's a grilled, natural-casing hot dog, loaded with chili sauce, mustard, and onions. Yummy...Detroit-style yummy!

Ingredients

  • 4 hotdogs with natural casings
  • 1 hot dog roll, sliced
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 1 (10 ounce) can of chile sauce without beans
  • 4 tablespoons of yellow mustard

Directions

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat.
  2. Place hot dogs on the grill; cook until browned, 5 to 8 minutes, turning once, or until done to suit your taste. Lightly grill hot dog rolls.
  3. Meanwhile, place the chile sauce in a small microwave-safe bowl; cook 1 minute. Stir, and cook 1 minute more.
  4. Place hot dogs on buns. Top each with chile sauce, onion, and 1 tablespoon mustard, or to taste.

Nutritional information

Per serving: 266 calories; protein 10.4g; carbohydrates 16.3g; fat 18g; cholesterol 30.2mg; sodium 877mg.
